Lasagna

Home

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics.

ingredients

  • Meat:
    • Italian sausage
    • lean ground beef
  • Onion: One onion
  • Garlic: Two cloves of garlic
  • Tomato products:
    • Crushed tomatoes: One can
    • Tomato sauce: Two cans
    • Tomato paste: Two cans
  • Sugar: Two table spoons
  • Spices and seasonings:
    • Fresh parsley
    • Dried basil leaves
    • salt
    • Italian seasoning
    • fennel seeds
    • black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles: Use store-bought or Homemade noodles
  • Cheeses:
    • Parmesan
    • Mozzarella
    • Ricotta cheese
  • Egg

Make Lasagna

  1. Make Meat Sauce

  2. Cook the noodles

  3. Make the Ricotta Mixture

  4. Layer the lasagna:

    This the order you should follow when layer your lasagna

    1. Meat sauce
    2. Noodles
    3. Ricotta mixture
    4. Mozzarella slices
    5. Meat sauce
    6. Parmesan cheese
    7. Repeat the layers then when done top with reaming parmesan

  5. Cover with foil and bake

  6. Let rest before serving
